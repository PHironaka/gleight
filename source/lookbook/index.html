Spring Collection

For our debut collection we escaped to the Hollywood Hills with photographer Henrik Purienne and muse Emily Labowe to capture the sunkissed, jetsetting lifestyle of Mr. Leight. Just as our eyewear evokes lifestyles, it also encompasses lifetimes â€“ of the Leight's travels and experiences, their knowledge of the industry, and the city they call home.

The Spring 2018 sun and eyeglasses feature custom acetates from the oldest and most respected Japanese houses inspired by vintage colors discovered in their archives. Precious metals shine throughout with unique tones developed exclusively for the collection. Innovative features like removable top bars and adjustable temples have been integrated into each frame, constructed and finished in Japan by factories that have worked with the Leight family for decades. This balance of legacy and luxury defines the collection and complements the aspirations and sophistication of Mr. Leight.
